    About this site

    Everything here is free, legal, and reproducible. Match results come from the CC0 international results dataset and the public-domain openfootball World Cup data (scores appear within minutes–hours of full-time, not in real time). Team strengths are Elo ratings recomputed from scratch over 49,000+ matches since 1872.

    The honest part: we pre-registered a fancier Poisson scoring model, tested it on the 2014/2018/2022 World Cups — and it lost to the simple Elo baseline, twice. So the baseline gets the headline and the fancy model is demoted to "experimental" scoreline heatmaps. The full story, math, and every logged decision: methodology. The entire prediction history is the repository's git log.

    The workflow part: this site is also a case study in agentic-ds Skill, a warrant-first data-science skill for making AI-assisted analysis reproducible, transparent, and auditable.

    Data: martj42/international_results (CC0-1.0) · openfootball/worldcup.json (public domain) · Map: Natural Earth (public domain) · Built with three.js · Not affiliated with FIFA · No betting advice — entertainment & education only.
